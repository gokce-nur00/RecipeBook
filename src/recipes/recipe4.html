Pita Dough Recipe

Ingredients for Pita Dough:

  • Two fingers less lukewarm water from 1 glass
  • 1 packet of yeast (two teaspoons)
  • 2 teaspoons of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 6 tablespoons of oil
  • Two fingers missing from 1 glass of yoghurt
  • 3 cups flour (approximately)

Note: If you want to make less, you can halve the ingredients. With these dough ingredients, five pita breads come out.

Minced Pita Stuffing Recipe

Ingredients for Minced Pita Stuffing:

  • 1 kg of ground beef (I also mixed 300 g of lamb)
  • 2 onions
  • 3 peeled tomatoes
  • half a bunch of parsley
  • Black pepper (two)
  • 1 spoon of chili powder, red pepper powder, salt
  • 1.5 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon of chili paste

Note: We made about 30 with this drink. Since our dough was too much, we finished the rest of the dough with sausage cheese.
