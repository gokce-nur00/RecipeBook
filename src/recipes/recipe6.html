Yayla Soup

Ingredients for Yayla Soup

  • 1 cup of rice (Turkish coffee cup)
  • 7 glasses of water
  • 1 cup of yogurt
  • 1 egg
  • A little more than half a cup of flour (Turkish coffee cup)
  • 3 tablespoons of oil
  • 2 spoonful butter
  • Salt
  • Mint

How to Make Plateau Soup Recipe?

  1. Water, oil, salt, and rice are put in the pot. The rice is boiled until soft.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk the yoghurt, egg, and flour together with 1 glass of water.
  3. When the rice becomes soft, mix it quickly and add the yogurt mixture to the soup. Stir until it boils. When it boils, it is boiled for 10-15 minutes on low heat. Don't forget to stir occasionally.
  4. After our soup is cooked, the bottom is closed.
  5. In terms of a stylish presentation; Soup is taken into bowls. Butter is heated in a separate place and mixed with mint. And it is drizzled over the soups in the bowls. Add the remaining mint butter to the soup in the pot and mix.

Our fragrant highland soup is ready to serve.
