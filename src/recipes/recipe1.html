Lentil Soup Recipe

Turkish red lentil soup is ready in 30 minutes and is best served with a squeeze of lemon. This gluten free and vegan soup is so simple to make at home.

Ingredients for Lentil Soup Recipe

  • 2 cups red lentils
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tablespoons of flour
  • 1 carrot
  • Half a tablespoon of pepper or tomato paste (you can increase it if you want it to be red or not use it at all)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Half a teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon cumin (optional)
  • 2 liters of hot water
  • 5 tablespoons of oil

For the sauce:

  • 2 spoonful butter
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
