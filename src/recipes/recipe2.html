Shepherd's Salad

Ingredients for Shepherd's Salad

  • 4 tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 green peppers
  • 1 cucumber
  • Parsley
  • Lemon juice
  • Olive oil

How to Make Shepherd's Salad Recipe?

  1. First, chop the tomatoes into small pieces and put them in a mixing bowl.
  2. Then chop the onion into small pieces, sprinkle salt on it and rub it well with your hands.
  3. Let's put our finely chopped green pepper and finely chopped cucumber into the mixing bowl.
  4. Finally, let's chop the parsley finely and add it to the other ingredients that we chop.
  5. After adding the olive oil and lemon juice of our salad, let's mix it well and serve it on the serving plate. Enjoy your meal.
